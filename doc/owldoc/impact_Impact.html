The class of impacts. An impact is a consequence. It may, for example, be a consequence of a large scale construction project, but there are many kinds of things that may have an impact. An impact has an impact on something. For example, an impact may have an impact on a community. However an impact can have an impact on a variety of different kinds of thing such as the environment or the economy.
