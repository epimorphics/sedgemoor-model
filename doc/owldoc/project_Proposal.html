rdfs:comment
The class of proposals. A distinction is made between a proposal and a document or set of documents that describe the proposal. Instances of the class :Proposal correspond to the abstract concept of 'what is proposed'. The wdrs:describedby property may be used to relate a proposal to one or more documents that describe the proposal that is made. The property community:community may be used to relate a proposal to a community to which the proposal is relevant. The property :relatedProposal may be used to relate a proposal to another, for example, to relate a proposal to one that was suggested by another agent.
